Module overview

The goal of this module is for you to get familiar with what kinds of methods are used for measuring brain activity and using it to address psycholinguistic questions. By the end of the module, hopefully you will be able to do the following things:

  • Explain the differences between some aspects of aphasiology, hemodynamics, and electrophysiology, as well as the advantages and disadvantages of each;
  • Discuss and analyze the meaning of neurolinguistic data patterns and what they suggest regarding psycholinguistic questions.

Note: many of the parts of this module were not covered in our class (2023), but they are here if you are interested in reading more.
