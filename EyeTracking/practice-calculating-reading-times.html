The busy girl saw the bank was closed that afternoon

I want to know how long people took to read the phrase was closed. Below I have listed several different reading time measures; some of them are ones you read about in the previous question, and some are totally new. (Beneath each one I have included a brief explanation of what it means.) Calculate each reading time measure and fill in the blank.

  • First fixation time: _____ ms
    • duration of the first fixation on this word or group of words
  • First pass time: _____ ms
    • sum of all fixations on this region, from the first time this region was looked at until the first time this region was left (in either direction)
  • Go-past time: _____ ms
    • sum of all fixations on any region, from the first time this region was looked at until the first time this region was gone past
  • Second-pass time: _____ ms
    • sum of all fixations on the region from the second time the region was looked at (after being left once) until the second time it was looked away from
  • Rereading time: _____ ms
    • sum of all fixations on the region after the region was gone past at least once
  • Total time: _____ ms
    • sum of all fixations on the region

When you have finished calculating your answers, you can check if they are correct on the bottom of the screen.

Once you've done these activities, continue to the next section of the module: "Reflection on eye-tracking for reading".

Answers: first fixation time: 706ms; first pass time: 1089ms; go-past time: 2133ms; second pass time: 111ms; rereading time: 545ms; total time: 1745ms.
