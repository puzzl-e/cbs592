Now let's consider one more thing.

Remember the pattern of results we found in a categorical experiment. When voice onset time is very short, people don't often think the sound is "pa". As voice onset time gets higher, that doesn't change much. When voice onset time reaches a certain point (a categorical boundary), suddenly things change; the line shoots up, and people almost always think the sound is "pa".
