First, let's do an experiment.

This experiment can be done online. To do the experiment, go to the web experiment and follow the instructions there.

(If for some reason the link does not work, please let me know. The usual solution is just to wait a while and try again.)

What do you think this experiment was about? What do you think the experiment wanted to test, and what results did it want to compare? What do you predict the results might show?

When you have finished these activities, continue to the next section of the module: "Reflection on the experiment".
